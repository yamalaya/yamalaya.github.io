About Us

We are a private institution in Nepal.
Our purpose is to help and guide those who seek to study and work abroad in Japan.

Our Services

  • We help you plan and prepare for your career in Japan.
  • We assist you through all the necessary documentation and processes required to obtain desired visa status.
  • We offer Japanese Language Proficiency Test(JLPT) preparation classes.
